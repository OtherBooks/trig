\(\triangle DBA\) and \(\triangle DBC.\) The hypotenuse is \(BC\) in \(\triangle ABC\text{,}\) \(BA\) in \(\triangle DBA\text{,}\) and \(AC\) in \(\triangle DAC\text{.}\) The short leg is \(AB\) in \(\triangle ABC\text{,}\) \(DB\) in \(\triangle DBA\text{,}\) and \(DA\) in \(\triangle DAC\text{.}\) The longer leg is \(AC\) in \(\triangle ABC\text{,}\) \(DA\) in \(\triangle DBA\text{,}\) and \(DC\) in \(\triangle DAC\text{.}\)
